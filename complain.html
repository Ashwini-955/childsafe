Why Report?

You're Protecting a Child

Children in labor situations are often subjected to physical and psychological harm. Your report can get them to safety.

Education, Not Labor

Every child deserves an education and a childhood free from exploitation. Your report helps make this possible.

100% Confidential

Your identity is protected. You can choose to remain anonymous when filing a report.

Quick Response

Our team works with local authorities to investigate reports and take appropriate action swiftly.

What Happens After You Report

Case Review

Our team reviews your report within 24 hours and categorizes it based on urgency and severity.

Investigation

We coordinate with local partners and authorities to investigate the reported situation.

Rescue Operation

If child labor is confirmed, a rescue operation is planned and executed to remove children from harm.

Rehabilitation

Rescued children receive medical care, counseling, and are enrolled in educational programs.

Legal Action

Legal proceedings against perpetrators are initiated through appropriate channels.
